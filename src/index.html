About

The Verdure Project, or Verdure for short, is the developer and maintainer of Verdure OS. Both the organization and its OS are still in the early stages of their creation. In the future, Verdure aims to innovate, develop new projects, help finance charities, and become a catalyst for change. More coming soon.

Innovate. Demonstrate. Organize.

Verdure OS

Verdure OS is an open-source operating system for personal computers and phones. It is currently still under heavy development. The goal of Verdure OS is to provide a clean, simple, and user-friendly experience.
