About

The Verdure Project, or Verdure for short, is developing and maintaining Verdure OS. Both this organization and its OS are still in the early stages of their creation. In the future, Verdure will innovate, develop new projects, help finance charity projects, and become a catalyst for change. More coming soon.

Innovate. Demonstrate. Organize.

Verdure OS

Verdure OS is an open-source operating system for personal computers and phones. Verdure OS is currently still under heavy development. The goal is to develop a clean, simple, and user-friendly OS experience.
